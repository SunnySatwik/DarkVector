1. The Vision
The Problem
Today's security software is overwhelming.
Analysts spend more time navigating dashboards than investigating threats.
There are:
•	dozens of widgets 
•	endless graphs 
•	hundreds of filters 
•	too many clicks 
•	poor explanations 
•	alert fatigue 
DarkVector exists to reduce cognitive load.
Instead of giving analysts more information, it helps them make better decisions.
________________________________________
Vision Statement
DarkVector is an AI-native security investigation platform that transforms raw telemetry into understandable investigations through intelligent reasoning, explainable machine learning, and beautifully designed workflows.
Notice something.
We never said
Dashboard.
